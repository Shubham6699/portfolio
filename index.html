About

From the shop floor to intelligent systems.

My foundation is mechanical engineering — 3D CAD in CATIA, ANSYS analysis, OEE studies, and quality systems on live production lines at Bosch and Rieter. That grounding in how factories actually run shapes everything I build today.

At Škoda / Volkswagen I moved into procurement analytics, building SAP-driven pipelines and reporting dashboards to steer sourcing decisions. At Mercedes-Benz U.S. International I built more than ten Power BI dashboards for logistics and yard operations, automated daily KPI reporting with Excel VBA, and helped develop AI-agent workflows for invoice auditing.

Since then I've gone deep on the modern automation stack — agentic workflows with n8n and Docker, RAG with local LLMs, Text-to-SQL, ROS2 perception with YOLO, and neural networks in TensorFlow — pairing engineering discipline with AI to make operations measurably better.
